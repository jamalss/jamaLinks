Loctote Bags

Loctote bags and/or accessories, in my opinion, are a must have for anybody and everybody who seeks the best value for their money. And when I say best, I mean the best. As stated on their website they push the boundaries of technical durability, modern innovation and timeless design. Below are a shortened list of some of the innovative features LOCTOTE products consist.

  1. Durable Fabrics: They are committed to crafting products that are built to last. Made with full grain leathers, durable waxed canvas and their signature cut-resistant fabrics — goods by Loctote are designed to endure. Buy once, use forever. Loctote uses a proprietary fabric made out of an innovative combination of ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene (UHMWPE) and other technical fibers weaved by special high-density knitting machines, creating a fabric that is unlike anything else anywhere.
  2. Reliable Safety: Anti-theft technology is found throughout our products. Loctote leverage ANSI-certified cut-resistant fabrics, stab-proof seams, steel reinforced straps, heavy-duty marine-grade locks and more.
  3. Cut-resistant rating: Their fabrics are rated on the ANSI Scale of cut-resistance. ANSI stands for the American National Standards Institute and is a private, nonprofit organization who have made it their lives’ work to create a national rating system for many things including cut-resistance.
  4. RFID Protection: A specialized internal pocket of the anti theft backpack is lined with RFID blocking material that will shield your phone, cards, and passport from unauthorized scanning devices.
  5. Water-Resistant Design: Important to note in some products, like the LOCTOTE backpacks, contain an understated drawstring design that will perfectly blend into any setting while the water-resistant exterior coating will protect your valuables from moisture.
